About us
Just a few words

Empowering Community Development Through Microfinance:
Global Investors Savings Bank
in Sri Lanka

We are committed to improving the standard of living for low-income Sri Lankan families by offering a variety of microfinance programs. Welcome to Global Investors Savings Bank in Sri Lanka. We are a bank committed to community development, and our varied clientele—which includes artists, students, fisherman, vendors, laborers, and business owners—embodies this commitment.

You will learn more about our organization and our unwavering dedication to providing superior, cutting-edge, and affordable financial solutions as you peruse the links below. Maximizing shareholder profits in the market is our main objective.

Our prestigious clientele, which includes Global Investors Savings Bank itself, the cooperative industry, and other community-based and non-governmental organizations, receives customized goods and services from us. We are using state-of-the-art banking technologies in conjunction with our current network to broaden our reach in order to accomplish this.

What sets us apart is our steadfast commitment to building a highly competent and driven team of experts. Their dedication to providing exceptional service is the foundation for our primary goals, which include safeguarding the bank's financial stability and promoting national growth.
